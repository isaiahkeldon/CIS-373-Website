Cardio

Cardio is a heavily overlooked aspect of fitness and overall health. Make sure to read up about it and incorporate it into your daily life. It can help prevent or mitigate many known diseases.

Learning

Sorry to say this but to apply long term changes to your life, you have to be willing to learn. Get ready to take some notes and review throughout your journey.

Resistance Training

Resistance training can be very helpful in ensuring your bone density doesn't decrease as you get older. It can also increase bone density as you do heavier weights. We highly recommend everyone to incorporate some form of resistance training into their life.

Nutrition

Nutrition is one of the biggest fundamentals in living a fit life. That means cutting out the processed meats, snacks, and fast food, and choosing a salad, cooking according to your diet, and drinking a lot of water.

Discipline & Motivation

Motivation will keep you wanting to make these everlasting changes in your life. Discipline will teach you to continue this journey by keeping up habits even when you don't want to do anything.

Tracking Progress

Nothing is more rewarding than realizing how far you have come, right? Well one way to measure your progress is by writing it down. It can help build motivation and help you discipline yourself by routinely engaging in tracking your progress.
